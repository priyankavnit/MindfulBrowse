How to Get Started

  1. Download: Click the download button above to save the extension file
  2. Extract: Unzip the downloaded file to any folder on your computer
  3. Open Chrome Extensions: Type chrome://extensions in your browser's address bar and press Enter
  4. Enable Developer Mode: Look for a toggle switch in the top-right corner and turn it on
  5. Load Extension: Click the "Load unpacked" button and select the folder where you unzipped the files
  6. Sign In: Click the Mindful Browse icon in your toolbar and log in with your account
  7. Start Browsing: That's it! The extension will now track your browsing patterns

After Installation

Once you're logged in, the extension works automatically in the background. You can:

  • Click the extension icon anytime to check your login status
  • Visit your dashboard to see insights about your browsing habits
  • Receive gentle notifications when unhealthy patterns are detected
