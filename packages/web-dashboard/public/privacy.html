🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 9, 2026

Our Privacy Promise: Mindful Browse is built with privacy at its core. We believe in transparency and giving you control over your data. This policy explains exactly what we collect, what we don't, and how we protect your information.

What We Collect

Data Type We Collect Details
Website URLs ✓ Yes Full URLs you visit (e.g., example.com/article-title)
Page Titles ✓ Yes Titles of pages you visit
Time Spent ✓ Yes How long you spend on each page
Scroll Activity ✓ Yes How much you scroll (to detect doomscrolling)
Timestamps ✓ Yes When you visit pages
Email Address ✓ Yes For account authentication only

What We DON'T Collect

Data Type We Collect Why Not
Page Content ✗ Never We never read or store what's on the pages you visit
Form Data ✗ Never We don't capture anything you type in forms
Passwords ✗ Never Your password is only used for authentication, never stored
Personal Messages ✗ Never We don't read your emails, chats, or private messages
Search Queries ✗ Never We don't track what you search for
Cookies/Tracking ✗ Never We don't use cookies or third-party trackers
Location Data ✗ Never We don't track your physical location
Device Information ✗ Never We don't collect device IDs or hardware info

How We Use Your Data

1. AI Analysis (Privacy-Preserving)

We use Amazon Bedrock AI to analyze:

  • Sentiment: Whether content is positive, negative, or neutral
  • Categories: Type of content (news, social media, entertainment, etc.)

The AI only sees URLs and titles - never the actual content of pages.

2. Pattern Detection

We analyze your browsing patterns to detect:

  • Doomscrolling behavior (excessive negative content consumption)
  • Information overload (too much content in short time)
  • Repetitive browsing (visiting same sites repeatedly)

3. Insights Generation

We create personalized insights about:

  • Your browsing time distribution
  • Content category preferences
  • Emotional patterns in your content consumption

Data Storage & Security

Where Your Data Lives

  • Cloud Storage: AWS (Amazon Web Services) in US East region
  • Database: Amazon DynamoDB with encryption at rest
  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest (AES-256)

Who Can Access Your Data

  • You: Full access to all your data via the dashboard
  • Our System: Automated processing only (no human review)
  • Third Parties: None - we never share or sell your data

Third-Party Services

We use the following AWS services to operate Mindful Browse:

  • Amazon Cognito: User authentication
  • Amazon DynamoDB: Data storage
  • Amazon Bedrock: AI analysis (Claude model)
  • AWS Lambda: Data processing
  • Amazon CloudFront: Content delivery

All services comply with AWS's security and privacy standards. We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • Sending an email notification for significant changes
  • Showing an in-app notification

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data:

  • Email: privacy@mindfulbrowse.com
  • General Support: support@mindfulbrowse.com
Remember: You're in control. You can stop metadata collection anytime by logging out or uninstalling the extension. Your privacy matters to us.
